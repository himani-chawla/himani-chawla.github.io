GDPR: WHAT IS CHANGING?
  • With the GDPR, the user consent must be given prior to the setting of the non-essential cookies, so that only strictly necessary cookies are initially set
  • Consenting to cookies needs to be a clear affirmative action
  • All given consents must be recorded as evidence
WHO IS IMPACTED
  • The Global Websites which have EU customers will get impacted and on those websites, no non-essential cookies or trackers shall be dropped onto the user's browser until consent has been given by the user to do so
